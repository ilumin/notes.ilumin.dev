# CAPEX

- CAPEX = Capital Expenditure
- company's major long term expenses
- exmaple: funds to buy physical assets, equipment, machine or build a new system
- paid upfront

# OPEX

- OPEX = Operating Expense
- company's dat-to-day expenses
- example: employee salaries, rent, bill
- paid weekly, monthly, or annaully
